Teaching Philosophy

Blending real-world engineering with academic excellence to shape Malaysia's future innovators through advanced teaching methodologies and industry-relevant curriculum.

My Teaching Philosophy

These six pillars form the foundation of my approach to engineering education, creating an environment where students can thrive academically and professionally in the rapidly evolving technological landscape.

Applied Engineering

Integrating real-world case studies, site visits, and laboratory experiences to ensure experiential learning. Connecting theoretical concepts with practical applications in electrical engineering, AI, and renewable energy.

Blended Innovation

Designing and implementing flipped classroom techniques and adaptive learning management systems for subjects like AI, IoT, and Data Analytics. Leveraging technology to enhance the learning experience.

Student Empowerment

Mentoring students from undergraduate projects to doctoral research with strong publication support. Having taught over 6,000 students, supervised 95 FYP students, and mentored numerous postgraduates.

Stakeholder Collaboration

Organizing Industry Advisory Panel meetings, industrial talks, and alumni engagement to ensure curriculum relevance. Maintaining strong connections with industry partners to keep students future-ready.

Tech-Driven Pedagogy

Leveraging AI, ML, IoT and practical tools from ongoing research and consultancy projects in teaching. Incorporating cutting-edge technologies to prepare students for the Fourth Industrial Revolution.

Professional Development

Encouraging continuous professional growth through certifications, industry engagement, and research. As a registered Professional Engineer (Ir.) with BEM and corporate member of IEM, I emphasize the importance of professional credentials.

Teaching Approach & Responsibilities

My comprehensive approach to education balances innovative teaching methods with core academic responsibilities.

Teaching Approach

  • Blended Learning Integration

    Developing LMS content with Blended Learning level 3 (Flipped Classroom) and level 4 (adaptive learning) for various engineering courses, enhancing student engagement and learning outcomes.

  • Laboratory Enhancement

    Upgrading undergraduate laboratories with projectors, smart screens, measurement instruments, 3D printers, and specialized kits to create immersive and practical learning environments.

  • Industry-Relevant Projects

    Incorporating real-world projects from my research and consultancy work in renewable energy, AI, and data analytics into the curriculum, providing students with authentic learning experiences.

  • Cooperative Learning

    Facilitating team-based learning environments where students collaborate on complex engineering problems, developing both technical skills and essential teamwork capabilities.

  • Emerging Technology Focus

    Emphasizing cutting-edge technologies like AI, IoT, renewable energy, and data analytics to prepare students for the rapidly evolving technological landscape and future career opportunities.

Key Responsibilities

  • Curriculum Development

    Developing and coordinating numerous engineering courses including Data Analytics for Engineers, Advanced Artificial Intelligence, and Introduction to Emerging Technologies, ensuring alignment with industry needs.

  • Student Mentorship

    Supervising 95 Final Year Project students, 52 internships, 6 masters and 3 PhD candidates, providing comprehensive guidance from undergraduate to postgraduate levels and supporting their research publications.

  • Research Leadership

    Leading 9 research grant projects worth RM 2.2 million in areas such as renewable energy, AI, and IoT, creating opportunities for students to participate in cutting-edge research with real-world applications.

  • Professional Certifications

    Maintaining numerous professional certifications in cybersecurity, data science, Python programming, and IoT development, demonstrating a commitment to continuous learning and professional excellence.

  • Stakeholder Engagement

    Serving as Head of Unit for Stakeholder Relations, organizing Industry Advisory Panel meetings, industrial talks, and alumni-staff meetings to ensure curriculum relevance and continuous improvement.
