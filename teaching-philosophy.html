Biography

Ir. Dr. Prajindra Sankar Krishnan

Senior Lecturer | AI & ML Researcher | Software Engineering Educator

Ir. Dr. Prajindra Sankar Krishnan is an experienced academic and professional engineer with over 15 years of experience in teaching, curriculum development, and research in the fields of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Software Engineering. He currently serves as a Senior Lecturer in the Department of Electronics & Communications at Universiti Tenaga Nasional (UNITEN), and is a Lead Researcher at the Institute of Sustainable Energy (ISE), where he spearheads AI-powered solutions for smart energy systems and predictive analytics.

His teaching portfolio spans undergraduate and postgraduate levels, covering a broad spectrum of computing subjects including AI, neural-fuzzy systems, data analytics, IoT programming, and software development. He has designed and moderated courses using advanced pedagogical strategies such as flipped classroom and adaptive learning, and is known for his ability to create engaging, industry-relevant learning environments. He also serves as a course coordinator and academic mentor, guiding students through research, internships, and professional development.

Dr. Prajindra actively contributes to academic service roles, including curriculum review, alumni-industry advisory boards, and quality enhancement through teaching evaluations and analytics-driven reporting. He is a certified Data Scientist, Python Programmer, Cybersecurity Specialist, and IIoT Developer, with strong command over platforms such as MATLAB, VB.Net, SQL, Android Studio, and TensorFlow.
